EUROPATOUR 2018

LØRDAG 29. SEPTEMBER KL. 20:00

KONSERT

Atmasfera is a multi-instrumental and melodious band combining indie music with ethno-motifs from the entire world - especially the enchanting mantras in Sanskrit. They have a unique intensity of sound in their dynamic melodies, which they also carry into their more tender and mellow pieces.

Atmasfera - Norge 2018

World Indie Concert

SATURDAY 29 SEPTEMBER 2018

Atmasfera combine indie music with ethno-motifs from the entire world. They have a unique intensity of sound in their dynamic melodies. In Atmasfera's music, you find an interesting fusion of modern western tendencies and ancient eastern culture, with a touch of Ukrainian folklore.

Yoga SoundFest

SUNDAY 30 SEPTEMBER 2018

Let's all come together, celebrate, and lighten up the darkness of autumn
with the ancient yoga sounds - that have the power to enliven our very being! Our special guests from Ukraine, indie yoga band Atmasfera, make this a completely magical experience of yoga, music, art, and fun.

Yoga Sound Retreat

2-7 OCTOBER 2018

A chance to meet more personally with mantra music band Atmasfera, and to take part in intimate mantra diving sessions that help you toward a state of deep inner peacefulness. The retreat will be held 4hrs from Trondheim by car, at a beautiful setting in Femundsmarka.
