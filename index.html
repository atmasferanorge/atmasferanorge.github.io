CONCERT

SATURDAY 29 SEPTEMBER | TRONDHEIM

INSPIRING INDIE FOLK MUSIC

"I feel warm from the inside, even though it's cold outside. The energy from the band... I will leave with a glow inside."

Sincere, striking, yet sublime - Atmasfera shares more than music. Their music style hybridizes ethnic elements, such as ancient mantras in Sanskrit, within a contemporary indie music frame. Similar bands include Fleet Foxes, Bon Iver, Beirut, Mumford & Sons, Of Monsters and Men, etc. Leading the band, the extraordinary twin sisters Yamuna and Kalindi inspire their audience around the world with heart-warming vocals and tunes from flute, keyboard, harmonium, and more. They are accompanied by Timur (drums, darbuka), Janardana (vocals, bass guitar), Dennis (acoustic guitar, dombra), and Sergiy (electric guitar).

YOGA SOUNDFEST

SUNDAY 30 SEPTEMBER | TRONDHEIM

Let's come together, celebrate, and lighten up the darkness of autumn with the ancient yoga sounds - that have the power to enliven our very being. Our guests from Ukraine, indie yoga band Atmasfera, make this a truly magical experience of yoga, music, and fun.

FREE TICKETS

Mantra Diving

Listen

Music is the heart & soul of Yoga SoundFest - in our main event called 'Mantra Diving', Atmasfera combine an indie music style with ancient Mantras for an energizing yet deeply relaxing music & meditation experience. We invite you to dance and sing along, or otherwise to just listen and embrace the magical mantra sounds in your heart.

Yoga & Meditation

Explore

Experience a whole new dimension to the yoga practice as we combine breath, movement, and live mantra music by Atmasfera. Our experienced yoga teachers will help you to energize and recharge your body with vinyasa flow. With their angel-like voices, Kalindi & Yamuna from Atmasfera will also lead a yoga sound & pranayama meditation session to bring peacefulness to your entire being.

Drum Circle

Take Part

In the spirit of the yoga lifestyle, we invite you to an interactive & fun learning experience with the skilled musicians of Atmasfera. With a unique and inspirational teaching style, Drummer Timur will share a spiritual approach to groove and lead you toward finding your own beat in our Drum Circle - a fun and inclusive workshop for both old and young.

Local & Sustainable Foods

Taste

Feeding your soul with the mantra sounds, we are sure you’ll be in need some healthy sustenance for your body. We have therefore invited passionate cooks to offer you a fresh selection of vegetarian & yoga-inspired foodstuffs and sweets. Surplus from food sales is used to keep this a free event, open to everyone.

ATMA RETREAT

2 - 7 OCTOBER | FEMUNDSMARKA

Find a deeper aspect to life - and get inspired to make a change! Together with yoga music band Atmasfera, we arrange mantra diving sessions, yoga asana classes, serve delicious vegetarian food, lead wilderness hikes, and much more.

TICKETS

Mantra Diving

Rest your heart in ancient yoga sounds

Weaving together melody, music, and mantra, Mantra Diving is a concept where participants respond to the words of the singer; clapping, swaying and sometimes dancing. In this way, it is simultaneously a meditative, relaxing, and fun activity. It is the most powerful and enjoyable form of meditation - a spiritual journey.

Yoga Asanas

Refresh and relax body & mind

Relax in body and mind with yoga exercises! We will teach hatha yoga centered around postures or asanas that aim to bring harmony between the body and the breath. Asana and pranayama (breathing exercises) open the subtle energy channels in the body, so that you can have a greater experience of vitality and life force in the body.

Wilderness Hiking

Explore Femundsmarka National Park

The retreat is located in clost proximity to Femundsmarka National Park, famous for its beautiful marshes and lakes.

Vegetarian Food

Enjoy daily home-made buffet meals

Feeding your soul with the mantra sounds, we are sure you’ll also be in need some healthy sustenance for your body. We therefore offer you a fresh selection of vegetarian & yoga-inspired foodstuffs and sweets.
